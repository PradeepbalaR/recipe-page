Pancakes

Description

These fluffy homemade pancakes are easy to make and perfect for breakfast. Serve them with butter, syrup, or your favorite toppings.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 tablespoons sugar
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 3/4 cup buttermilk
  • 1 egg
  • 2 tablespoons melted butter
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Steps

  1. In a bowl, mix flour, sugar, baking powder, baking soda, and salt.
  2. In another bowl, whisk buttermilk, egg, melted butter, and vanilla.
  3. Combine wet and dry ingredients, mixing until just combined.
  4. Heat a pan over medium heat and lightly grease with butter.
  5. Pour batter onto the pan, cooking until bubbles form on the surface.
  6. Flip and cook the other side until golden brown.
  7. Serve warm with syrup and butter. Enjoy!
