Lasagna Recipe page

This is the recipe page for lasanga

Ingredients:

  • 9 lasagna noodles
  • 1 lb (450g) ground beef or Italian sausage
  • 1 small onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 (24 oz) jar pasta sauce
  • 1 (15 oz) container ricotta cheese
  • 1 egg
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • ½ cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 tsp dried Italian seasoning
  • Salt & pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven:to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cook lasagna noodles: according to package instructions; drain and set aside.
  3. Cook meat: In a pan, sauté onion and garlic, then add ground beef. Cook until browned, drain excess fat.
  4. Add sauce: Stir in the pasta sauce, season with Italian seasoning, salt, and pepper. Simmer for 5 minutes.
  5. Prepare ricotta mixture: In a bowl, mix ricotta cheese, egg, and half the Parmesan cheese.
  6. Assemble lasagna: In a baking dish, layer as follows:
    • Spread a little sauce on the bottom
    • Add 3 noodles
    • Spread some ricotta mixture
    • Sprinkle mozzarella cheese
    • Repeat layers until all ingredients are used, ending with sauce & cheese on top.
  7. Bakefor 30–40 minutes until bubbly and golden.
  8. let it restfor 10 minutes before slicing. Enjoy!
